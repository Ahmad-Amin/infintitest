The
Challenge

Complete design and development of a mobile application

Separate intuitive interfaces for shifters and venues

Uber-type model to connect both interfaces using specific algorithms

Seamless payment integration and rating system to build a strong community

Shifters
Interface

We developed an app that allows shifters to create their profiles, access countless opportunities, keep track of their earnings, and get their strong reputation among the hospitality industry.

Venues
Interface

We developed a separate interface for the venues that allows them to create their profiles, improve their services by hiring the right part-time workers for specific occasions, and have tangible proof of the shifters experience through previous ratings.

Illustrations

We have created different types of illustrations to bond users and align it to its existing branding.
