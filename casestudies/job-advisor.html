The
Challenge

Complete design and development of a user-friendly web platform

5 steps registration process for job seekers with an in built CV preview

Integration of AI to help companies find their ideal candidates based on their recruitments needs

Allowing companies to extract contact details, initiate online chat, and conduct interviews with candidates

Allowing candidates to view the statistics of their profile, and have access to educational and industry updates

Website
Interface

We have developed a website where users get to see how our recruitment platform works, the name of companies that are hiring, as well as educational content to guide them through their hiring journey.

Job Seeker
Web Platform

We have developed a separate interface for the job seekers to create their profile using our 5 step registration process, view their live statistics and companies looking at their profile, as well as getting access to educational and industry updates that are of interests to them.
