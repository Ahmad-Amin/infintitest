STORE2DOOR

Facilitating the buying processes in restricted countries.

Story

Store2Door is a courier company in Saudi Arabia.
They provide a shipping service that delivers products from countries
that usually do not ship to KSA by giving users a physical address in the
store’s country that they can use to order. Store2Door then takes care of
getting the product from this physical warehouse to the buyer’s home.

We have redesigned and redeveloped the web application to improve UX. We have also added an assisted purchase module that allows users to check-out almost instantly by automatically processing the products at their chosen eCommerce URL.
